🌾 About Keheilan

The trust gap

Egyptian agriculture is a $40B sector that retail capital can't reach. Buyers don't trust farmer self-reporting. Investors can't verify land quality. The result: capital sits in real estate while farmland underperforms.

Keheilan closes the gap by making AI the objective third-party auditor. Land health, valuations, risk tiers, and operations are all scored by models that take real data — not paperwork.

The three engines

Model 1 — Fractional Land. The asset. AI scores parcels, investors buy shares (1000 per parcel), farmers lease at company-set rents. Lease revenue auto-splits to shareholders.

Model 2 — Farm Operations. The funding. Multi-stage agentic crop advisor: parses farm context, plans queries, gathers evidence (search + soil + weather), scores crops on agronomic + economic + risk dimensions, self-critiques, and writes a justification.

Model 3 — Hybrid. Dynamic capital rebalancing between land and operations. Planned for v2.
